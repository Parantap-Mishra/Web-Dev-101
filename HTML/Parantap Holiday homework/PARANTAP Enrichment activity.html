Covid19-India's Fight

What is Covid 19?

The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).
The World Health Organization declared the outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern on 30 January, and a pandemic on 11 March.
As of 27th August 2020, more than 24.3 million cases of COVID-19 have been reported in more than 213 countries and territories, resulting in more than 8,28,000 deaths; more than 15.9 million people have recovered.
The virus is primarily spread between people during close contact,most often via small droplets produced by coughing, sneezing, and talking.
The droplets usually fall to the ground or onto surfaces rather than travelling through air over long distances.

India's Fight Against COVID-19

a) Role of Government

  1. The Indian Government had declared a nationwide lockdown at the correct time to contain coronavirus.
    • Phase 1: 25 March 2020 to 14 April 2020
    • Phase 2: 15 April 2020 to 3 May 2020
    • Phase 3: 4 May 2020 to 17 May 2020
    • Phase 4: 18 May 2020 to 31 May 2020
    • Phase 5 (only for containment zones): 1 June 2020 to 30 June 2020
  2. The Government launched an app called 'Arogya Setu' that notified us if there is a Corona case near us.
  3. The Government also launched a fund known as 'The PM Cares Fund' in which we could donate our money for thr poor and needy in this Pandemic.



b) Role of Medical Personnel

Doctors,nurses,medical staff and lab technicians work over time to cure the covid patients and risk their lives to save ours.


c) Role of Police

The police is risking their lives and making sure everyone is following lockdown and social distancing.

d) Others

  1. The sweepers and cleaners also risk their live to clean and disinfect the societies and areas.
  2. Many people have also shown humanity by generously donating their money to the poor and needy in these difficult times.
  3. The citizens have understood the severity of this situation and are maintaining the norms of lockdown. Hence, controlling the spread of coronavirus.
